**Learning objective:** By the end of this lesson, students will combine what they've learned about cloud service models, major providers, and your own business needs into a simple presentation.

## Exercise

Build a presentation that demonstrates your understanding of the cloud concepts and providers you've learned about in this lab. Complete this exercise in your Google Slides presentation.

At a minimum, your presentation should include:

- An introductory slide with your name and the title of your presentation.

- A slide explaining IaaS, PaaS, and SaaS in your own words.

- A slide showing the logos of AWS, Azure, and GCP, with a few notes on what makes each one unique.

- A slide listing the 3-5 most important things for a business when choosing a cloud provider.

- Finally, you'll pick a single service from one of the providers and create multiple slides that show:

  - What the service is.
  - How it works.
  - What makes it useful for a business.

  You can use the information you found in the scavenger hunt exercise in the Exploring Major Cloud Service Providers lesson, or pick a different service that you are interested in exploring. These slides should represent the bulk of your presentation.

As you build your presentation, remember to keep it simple and clear. Use bullet points, images, and diagrams to make your points easy to understand. Avoid cluttering your slides with too much text. Your slides should support your presentation and be a visual aid, not a script.

Be mindful of the time limit you'll have to present your slides. Rehearse your presentation to ensure you can explain each slide in a clear and concise manner, and be ready to answer any questions your audience may have.
