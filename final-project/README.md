**Learning objective:** By the end of this lesson, students will combine what they've learned about cloud service models, major providers, and your own business needs into a simple presentation.

# Exercise

Make a short presentation that includes:

- A slide explaining IaaS, PaaS, and SaaS in your own words
- A slide showing the logos of AWS, Azure, and GCP, with a few notes on what makes each one special
- A slide listing the 3-5 most important things for your business when choosing a cloud provider, with a sentence or two explaining each one
- A slide with your picture from Exercise 4, showing how a request goes from a user to a cloud service and back
- Be ready to explain each slide in simple terms.
