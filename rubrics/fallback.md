## Lab requirements

The following requirements describe the necessary deliverables for the Cloud Concepts and Providers Lab.

| Criteria                                                                                                                                                                                      | Points |
| --------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------- | ------ |
| Your name and the presentation's title are present on an introductory slide.                                                                                                                  | 5      |
| Accurately define IaaS, PaaS, and SaaS in your own words.                                                                                                                                     | 10     |
| Provide accurate and unbiased information about what makes AWS, Azure, and GCP unique.                                                                                                        | 10     |
| Include 3-5 accurate and unbiased key factors to note when choosing a provider.                                                                                                               | 10     |
| A single service from a provider is selected and the presentation contains accurate information about what that service is, how it works, and the features that make it useful to a business. | 25     |
| The slide content supports the verbal presentation instead being a script.                                                                                                                    | 10     |
| Your presentation is delivered to the class at the assigned date and time.                                                                                                                    | 15     |
| The presentation is delivered within the time limit provided.                                                                                                                                 | 10     |
| Visual elements and design in the presentation is professional.                                                                                                                               | 5      |
| **Total possible points**                                                                                                                                                                     | 100    |
